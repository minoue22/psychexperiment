Media use Questionnaire(< 10 minutes)

Thank you for visiting this page.We would appreciate it if you could spare several minutes to complete a short survey after reading the following information.

The survey is exploring the amount of time you use media and the extent to which you use different media concurrently on a typical WEEKDAY. The survey consists of two parts:

  • In the first part,you will be presented with a list of 12 media. For each medium,you will estimate the total amount of time you spend on the medium on a typical WEEKDAY.
  • The second part will only include the media that you spend some time using them on a typical WEEKDAY. For each medium you see on a typical WEEKDAY,you will use the scale below to indicate how often you concurrently use each of of the remaining media while using it as the primary activity on a typical WEEKDAY.

    • never,  A little of time,   Some of the time,   or   Most of the time
